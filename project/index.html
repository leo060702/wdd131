Welcome to Yosemite Road Trip Guide

Your essential companion for planning a scenic and memorable road trip to Yosemite National Park.

Yosemite National Park is one of the most famous national parks in the United States, located in the Sierra Nevada mountains of Central California. It features some of the world’s oldest giant sequoia groves, majestic granite cliffs shaped like sculptures, the iconic Tunnel View, and breathtaking waterfalls. Yosemite is ideal for both road trips and hiking adventures, offering different scenery in each season—lush greenery in spring, clear blue skies in summer, golden leaves in autumn, and snowfall in winter.
